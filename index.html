NIT SILCHAR

NIT Silchar is an autonomous university established as a joint venture between Govt of India and State Govt of Assam.
The institute ranks at 38th in the Engineering list of NIRF 2022.

NIT SILCHAR COURSES

Courses offered at NIT Silchar include BTech, MBA, MTech and Ph.D. NIT Silchar BTech is offered at the undergraduate level for 4-years of duration. The institute offers 2-years MTech, MBA, and MSc programs across a variety of specialisations in engineering, sciences and management.

B.Tech

The duration of the programme leading to B.Tech.degree is four years. The curricula for the different degree programmes as proposed by the respective departments and recommended by the DUPC shall have theapproval of the Senate.The curricula for B.Tech.programme includes compulsory Industrial training of 6-8 weeks duration after 6th semester in any reputed industry, research organization, IIT's and other reputed institutions which is assessed in the 7th Semester.

M.Tech

NIT SILCHAR offers a regular Master of Technology (MTech) programme of two-year duration in two categories, i.e. full-time sponsored and part-time.The 3rd and 4th semester mostly constitute the project work for M.Tech.Project work and Seminar are essential part of the curricula.Class tests,tutorials,laboratory assignments etc., are the constituent components of continuous assessment process and a student must fulfil all these requirements as prescribed.

MBA/Ph.D

The research programmes (MBA and Ph.D.) train the candidates to pursue original research ideas and augment the existing knowledge. It prepares them for academic as well as research and development careers. Admission to Doctor of Philosophy is based on the following categories. Our Facilities

The Institute has a playground with facility for playing Football, Cricket, Hockey and a Sports Complex for providing various types of outdoor and indoor games. The workshop of the Mechanical Engineering Department acts as a Central Workshop of the Institute. A fully computerized full-fledged and digitalized library in the campus. The Institute has got a Sub Post Office within the campus.The Sub Post Office has facilities like registration,money order and speed post.

World Class Digital library

The Library is a part of the LTT building & having around 97,000 collections of documents of both print & non print materials.

Play Ground

Well maintained playgrounds and kits as required for various sports like Football, Cricket, Kabbadi, Kho-Kho are available.

Hostel Facilities

The Institute has got 8 Boys hostels and 3 Girls hostels.All the hostels have their individual MMC to look after the fooding requirement for their boarders.
